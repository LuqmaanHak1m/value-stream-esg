Overview

LSEG Sustainability Ratings and Data simplifies a complex landscape of ESG corporate reporting, regulatory requirements and investor needs. It’s designed to enable practical insights that support capital allocation decisions, benchmarking and engagement.

Our flagship ESG data solution comprises standardised sustainability data, ESG materiality assessments to capture real-world risk exposure, and trusted ESG scores that aggregate insights into clear, actionable sustainability signals.

    Our solution

    LSEG Sustainability Ratings and Data is organised into three categories to enable customers to meet their individual investing or financing requirements.

    Dynamic and comprehensive ESG scores

    Our scoring framework aggregates insights into a suite of sustainability signals measuring a company’s absolute ESG management quality and performance. It includes theme-level scores across 12 ESG themes as well as overall ESG scores.

    For deeper insight, ESG Scores Plus integrates risk metrics – such as corporate controversies and sovereign ESG risks – and impact metrics – including green revenues and ESG debt issuance, offering a holistic view of corporate sustainability that goes beyond traditional ESG assessments.

    ESG materiality assessments

    To ensure ESG risks are evaluated through both a financial and real-world impact lens, we have developed a sustainability - focused materiality matrix that combines a redesigned industry classification with a double materiality framework.

    This helps assess companies’ exposure to ESG risks by mapping the relevance of key sustainability themes across industry sectors and assigning each one a classification of Not Relevant, Relevant, Material or Critical.

    Standardised sustainability data

    We curate publicly available data and transform it into structured, comparable ESG metrics through rigorous quality control and analysis. Our concise set of high-quality indicators is aligned with global standards, delivering insights that you can trust. Our data comprises:

    • 170 tightly defined ESG metrics collected and standardised from public disclosures
    • 23 climate indicators from the Transition Pathway Initiative Management Quality methodology
    • over 50 additional data points from media sources including Reuters news, fixed income data from LSEG and the Climate Bonds Initiative and macroeconomic datasets such as those from the United Nations and the World Bank

     

    Why it’s unique

    Redesigned ESG foundations

    • A curated set of high-quality ESG indicators focusing on framework alignment, metric quality, data availability and concise design.
    • A sustainability-first materiality matrix, combining a redesigned industry classification with a double materiality approach to assess companies’ exposure to ESG risks.

    Next level scoring model

    • A 0 to 5 scoring methodology designed to gauge the quality of companies’ management of sustainability risks and opportunities.
    • Each ESG theme contains unique capping metrics that ensure key aspects of ESG strategies are required to progress in the scoring.
    • Performance metrics in specific ESG themes reward quantifiable and real-world progress on sustainability outcomes.

    Modular ESG architecture

    • Standalone ESG theme assessments that can be used independently or be flexibly aggregated into customised composite scores.
    • A benchmarking-focused scoring system to enable comparison between companies, sectors and geographies.
    • LSEG ESG Scores Plus - integrating Risk and Impact factors such as contribution to the green economy, ESG debt financing, corporate controversies and sovereign ESG risks.

    Delivery channels

    LSEG’s sustainable finance and investment data is integrated across a range of proprietary and third-party platforms, including cloud services, allowing customers flexible access. LSEG Sustainability Ratings and Data can be accessed via:

    • LSEG Workspace: Offers comprehensive ESG insights, news and analytics, with real-time and historical data accessible through a user-friendly interface integrated with Microsoft 365 and HERE (formerly OpenFin).
    • LSEG Datastream: A global database for historical macroeconomic, financial and sustainable finance data, Supports rigorous analysis and modelling, with custom data and commercials available in multiple formats, such as CSV, JSON and Python.
    • Cloud delivery: Enables seamless data sharing and collaboration across cloud partners like Microsoft Azure, AWS and Snowflake. Data is provided in formats such as SQL databases and bulk feeds, simplifying processing and reducing operational tasks.
    • LSEG SQL endpoint, APIs and bulk services: Allow scalable ingestion of large datasets into clients’ internal systems via API, SQL or direct bulk file integration, including ESG point-in-time data for strategy back-testing.
    • LSEG Quantitative Analytics: Delivers standardised, point-in-time data for quantitative research through Snowflake, Azure, or as a deployed database, supporting content for global pricing data, economic and company data from us and third-party providers.

    Contributor tool

    Our ESG Contributor Tool helps you verify and provide timely updates on your ESG initiatives to investors and other stakeholders. It enables you to showcase a deep range of environmental, social and governance data from your firm, allowing you to:

    • Ensure the financial community has access to the timeliest ESG data on your company
    • Review and edit the historical ESG data that is already available for your company
    • Communicate more effectively with your stakeholders

    Codes of conduct for ESG Ratings and Data Products Providers

    LSEG fully supports global initiatives aimed at improving the quality, reliability, and transparency of ESG scores and data. In line with our commitment to these principles, we have endorsed a wide range of ESG Codes of Conduct:

    Country/entity Code of conduct Statements of compliance
    Japanese Financial Services Agency Code of Conduct for ESG Evaluation and Data Providers Access LSEG’s Statement of Compliance with the Japanese Financial Services Agency’s Code of Conduct for ESG Evaluation and Data Providers
    International Capital Market Association (ICMA) and International Regulatory Strategy Group (IRSG) Code of Conduct for ESG Ratings and Data Products Providers Access LSEG’s Statement of Compliance with the Code of Conduct for ESG Ratings and Data Products Providers by ICMA and IRSG
    Monetary Authority of Singapore Code of Conduct for ESG Rating and Data Product Providers Access LSEG’s Statement of Compliance with the Singapore Code of Conduct for ESG Rating and Data Product Providers
    Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission Code of Conduct for ESG Ratings and Data Products Providers Access LSEG’s Statement of Compliance with the Hong Kong Code of Conduct for ESG Ratings and Data Products Providers

    With its position at the intersection of global markets and businesses, LSEG is actively involved in the European financial ecosystem. We are committed to apply for ESG rating provider authorisation from ESMA in 2026 and continue to service the industry with its market leading ESG scores and data solutions.

    Discontinuation of ESG scores and ratings available on our website to India based users

    LSEG is no longer distributing ESG scores and ratings to our customers and users in India. ESG rating providers are required to obtain a license to provide ESG scores and ratings to customers and users in India. LSEG is not currently pursuing an application to obtain the aforesaid license. 

    To this end, users in India are unable to view ESG scores and ratings available on our website. Please note that any ESG scores and ratings appearing on our website are not meant or suitable for India based users.
